Common Questions

What People Ask

The questions that keep you up at night. Answered honestly.

Is SafeMind actually run by humans, not bots? +
Yes. Every response, every session, every piece of advice comes from a real person. We use AI tools to organize information, but the thinking, empathy, and guidance is 100% human. If you want to talk to a bot, there are plenty of those elsewhere.
Is this therapy? Do I need a diagnosis? +
No diagnosis needed. SafeMind is career support with a psychological lens — we help you navigate AI anxiety, build skills, and protect your job. If you need clinical therapy, Dr. Elena offers that too. But most people just need clarity, a plan, and someone who gets it.
What if I've already been laid off because of AI? +
Then you're in the right place. We have specific protocols for post-layoff recovery: reframing your experience, identifying transferable skills, building a job search strategy that positions your "AI encounter" as an asset, not a liability. You're not starting from zero.
Will AI really take my job, or am I overreacting? +
It depends on your role. Some jobs are genuinely at risk. Others are safer than the headlines suggest. We analyze your specific situation — your industry, your tasks, your company — and give you an honest assessment. No hype, no false reassurance. Just facts and a plan.
How soon will I see results? +
Most people feel more in control within the first week. The 7-day guide gives immediate quick wins. Within 30 days, you'll have new skills, new confidence, and a clear positioning at work. The fear doesn't vanish overnight — but it stops controlling you.
Is the free guide just a teaser, or actually useful? +
It's genuinely useful. The free guide gives you 7 days of concrete actions, role-specific tools, and immediate wins. The paid plan goes deeper — 30-day roadmaps, templates, scripts, and personal support. But the free version alone will save you hours and reduce your anxiety.
What makes SafeMind different from career coaching? +
Traditional career coaches don't understand AI. AI courses don't understand your fear. We do both. We combine workplace strategy with psychological support — because the biggest barrier to adapting to AI isn't technical, it's emotional.
Can my company pay for SafeMind for employees? +
Yes. We offer corporate packages for teams going through AI transitions. Companies that invest in their people's AI resilience see higher retention, faster adoption, and better morale. Email us at hello@safemind.pro for corporate rates.
