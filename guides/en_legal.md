# SafeMind — Survival Guide in the Age of AI

## For Legal Professionals

### Introduction
AI analyzes contracts, searches precedents, generates documents. A lawyer who uses AI makes 5x more deductions in the same time.

### Threats
- AI analyzes contracts in minutes, not days
- Automatic precedent search across databases
- Standard document generation without lawyers
- AI predicts court case outcomes

### Opportunities
- Analyze 1000 pages of documents per hour
- Instant relevant precedent search
- Automatic contract risk checking
- Court outcome prediction

### Actions Now
1. Try Harvey AI or CoCounsel for document analysis
2. Use ChatGPT for initial contract analysis
3. Implement AI for precedent search
4. Automate standard document generation

- Week 2: AI for precedent search
- Week 3: AI for document generation
