# SafeMind — Survival Guide in the Age of AI

## For Transport and Logistics Professionals

### Introduction
AI optimizes routes, predicts breakdowns, manages warehouses. A logistics professional without AI is a dispatcher with a map in the GPS era.

### Threats
- AI plans routes better than experienced dispatchers
- Automatic warehouse management (robots + AI)
- Predictive vehicle maintenance
- Autonomous vehicles

### Opportunities
- Route optimization with 100+ parameters
- Predict breakdowns before they happen
- Automatic inventory management
- AI for supplier and carrier selection

### Actions Now
1. Try AI for route planning
2. Implement predictive maintenance
3. Use AI for inventory management
4. Automate supplier selection

### 30-Day Plan
- Week 1: AI for routing
- Week 2: AI for predictive maintenance
- Week 3: AI for inventory management
- Week 4: Logistics process automation
