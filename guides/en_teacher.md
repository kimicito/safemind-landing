# SafeMind — Survival Guide in the Age of AI

## For Teachers

### Introduction
AI is transforming education: personal tutors, automatic grading, learning material generation. A teacher who uses AI delivers 3x more value.

### Threats
- AI tutors replace group classes
- Automatic essay and test grading
- Curriculum generation without human input
- Students use ChatGPT for homework

### Opportunities
- Personalized learning trajectories
- Automatic grading of routine assignments
- Interactive material generation
- AI helps struggling students without distracting advanced ones

### Actions Now
1. Try ChatGPT for lesson preparation
2. Use AI for test generation
3. Implement AI tutoring as a supplement
4. Teach students to work WITH AI, not against it

### 30-Day Plan
- Week 1: AI for lesson preparation
- Week 2: AI for test and assignment creation
- Week 3: AI for student feedback
- Week 4: AI integration into learning process
