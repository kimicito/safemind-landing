# SafeMind — Survival Guide in the Age of AI

## For HR Professionals

### Introduction
AI already screens resumes, conducts initial interviews, analyzes employee engagement. An HR professional who doesn't use AI spends 80% of their time on routine tasks.

### Threats
- AI resume screening (90% filtered automatically)
- Chatbots conduct initial interviews
- Analytics predict resignations
- Automatic onboarding of new employees

### Opportunities
- AI selects candidates more accurately than humans
- Real-time engagement analysis
- Personal development plans for every employee
- Routine automation: vacations, certificates, reports

### Actions Now
1. Try AI for resume screening (Hiire, XOR)
2. Use ChatGPT for job description creation
3. Implement engagement analytics (Glint, Peakon)
4. Automate onboarding

### 30-Day Plan
- Week 1: AI in recruitment
- Week 2: AI in onboarding
- Week 3: Engagement analytics
- Week 4: HR process automation
