# SafeMind — Survival Guide in the Age of AI

## For Economists

### Introduction
AI analyzes macroeconomics, models scenarios, predicts trends. An economist without AI is an analyst with Excel in the Big Data era.

### Threats
- AI models the economy more accurately than traditional methods
- Automatic data collection and analysis
- AI predicts crises before analysts
- Algorithmic trading changes markets

### Opportunities
- Analyze millions of data points per second
- Scenario modeling of economic shocks
- AI for inflation and GDP forecasting
- Automatic economic report generation

### Actions Now
1. Try AI for macroeconomic analysis
2. Use AI for scenario modeling
3. Implement AI for trend forecasting
4. Automate economic data collection

### 30-Day Plan
- Week 1: AI for data collection
- Week 2: AI for trend analysis
- Week 3: AI for scenario modeling
- Week 4: Economic report automation
